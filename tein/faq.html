Q: How much protein can I have per meal?
A: The human body can only take 20-40 grams of protein in one meal or sitting.


Q: How much protein can I have each day?
A: For men, it is recommended to take 0.8 gram of protein per pound of body weight to maintain mass. And, 1.5 grams of protein per pound of body weight if you're trying to gain mass.
For women, it is recommended to take 0.8 gram of protein per pound of body weight to maintain mass. And, 1.2 grams of protein per pound of body weight if you're trying to gain mass.

Q: What if I want to gain mass, but I can't have that much protein in a day?
A: Then, consider the your calculated amount for maintaining mass rather than gaining mass. The (maintain - maximum gain) range gives you a better idea of the minimum and maximum amounts of protein you could take in order to see muscle growth.
For example, "(180g of protein - 270g of protein) is the
(minimum - maximum) range for my daily amount of protein."
